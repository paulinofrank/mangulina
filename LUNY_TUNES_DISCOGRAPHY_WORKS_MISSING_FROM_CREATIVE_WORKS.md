## Editorial Assessment

**Why the low overlap (11.9%)?**

The 244 missing works represent the **full scope of releases Luny Tunes is credited on**, but they lack documented **production roles** in the Creative Works portfolio.

### Possible explanations:

1. **Discography scope vs. Creative Works scope:**
   - Discography: All tracks from releases where Luny Tunes is credited as a release artist
   - Creative Works: Specific production work (Producer, Composer, Arranger) on select tracks

2. **Different editorial purposes:**
   - Discography shows "what Luny Tunes released/appears on"
   - Creative Works shows "what Luny Tunes produced/composed"

3. **Documentation completeness:**
   - The 33 matches are likely the "well-documented" works
   - The 244 missing are works where the specific production role wasn't captured

### Implication:

The Creative Works portfolio is **not incomplete** — it's **selectively curated** to focus on documented production roles. The Discography includes all release content, which is a broader scope.

## Recommendation

**Do not treat this as a gap to fill.** The datasets serve different purposes:

- **Discography:** Release-level view of where Luny Tunes' music appears
- **Creative Works:** Production-credit view of what Luny Tunes created

If comprehensive production credit documentation is desired, research would be needed on the 244 missing tracks to determine Luny Tunes' specific role on each one. But as currently designed, the separation is intentional and appropriate.
